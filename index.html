Redmond, WA, August 16, 2023 - Inc. magazine announced that MAQ Software is honored on the prestigious Inc. 5000 list for the eleventh time. Inc., the leading magazine for mid-market companies, has been ranking the 5000 fastest growing private companies annually since 2007. The list represents a unique look at the most successful companies within the American economy’s most dynamic segment — independent businesses. MAQ Software was first honored in 2007. In the past 17 years, 35,282 companies have appeared on the Inc. 5000 list. Only 213 companies (less than 0.60%) demonstrated sustained growth to appear on the list eleven or more times. “We thank our customers for trusting in our team to deliver innovative solutions consistently,” says MAQ Software Founder and CEO, Rajeev Agarwal, “I also want to thank our team for their agility and flexibility in adopting new technologies. The Inc. 5000 list is a great honor. Our customers know they can build long-term partnerships with us because we build great software.”
